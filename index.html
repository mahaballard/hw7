Homework 7: Make a choropleth or proportional symbol map

In this assignment, I chose to create a choropleth and symbol map of the current voter registration status in Colorada as of February 1st, 2022. I chose this topic because there are several local and state elections that will take place in the next couple months and I wanted to see how many people are registered and their status whether they are active, inactive or preregistered to vote!
